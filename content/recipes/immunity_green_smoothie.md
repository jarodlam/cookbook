### Ingredients

* 1 cup coconut water
* 2 leaves romaine lettuce
* 1/2 green apple (Granny Smith)
* 1/4 cucumber
* 1/4 avocado, peeled
* 1/2 lemon, peeled
* 1/2 inch slice fresh ginger
* 1/2 cup fresh parsley
* 1 serving probiotic powder
* 3-4 ice cubes (optional)

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
