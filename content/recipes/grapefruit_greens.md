### Ingredients

* 1 cup water
* 1 cup or 4 large leaves romaine lettuce
* 1 stalk celery
* 1/3 cucumber
* 1/2 grapefruit, peeled
* 1/2 lime, peeled
* 1/3 cup coriander leaves

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
