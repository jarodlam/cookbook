## Ingredients
* Rempah
  * Onion
  * Chilli
  * Lemongrass
  * Galangal
  * Turmeric
  * Belacan
  * Dried shrimp
* Coconut milk
* Coconut cream
* Vegetables
  * Small tofu
  * Fish balls
  * Bean curd sheet
  * Cabbage
  * Long bean
  * Carrot
  * Eggplant

## Method
1. Grind rempah ingredients together. Grind the dried shrimp separately.
2. Fry rempah. Add dried shrimp.
3. Add coconut milk, cook until boiling.
4. Add vegetables and cook until tender.
5. Add coconut cream.
