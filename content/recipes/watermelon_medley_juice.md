### Ingredients

* 1 1/2 cups watermelon
* 1/2 cucumber
* 1 cup kale (3-4 small leaves with stems)
* 6 mint leaves
* 1 inch slice fresh ginger

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
