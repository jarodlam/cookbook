Makes 2 cakes of a reasonable size.

## Ingredients
* 3 x 500 g packets of glutinous rice flour
* 900 g sugar
* 6 cups warm water

## Method
1. Fry sugar until dissolved. Add warm water to dilute. Let cool.
2. Mix in glutinous flour.
3. Steam for approximately 2 hours each.
