### Ingredients

* 1 1/2 cups coconut water
* 1/2 cup kale
* 1/2 cup spinach
* 1/2 cup romaine lettuce
* 1/2 lime, peeled
* leaves from 2 stems of mint

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
