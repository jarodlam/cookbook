### Ingredients

* 1 cup homemade Brazil nut milk
* 1 cup or 4 kale leaves
* 1 banana
* 1/2 cup frozen strawberries
* 1 tbl goji berries

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
