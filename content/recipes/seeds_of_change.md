### Ingredients

* 1 cup almond milk 
* 1 tbl hemp seeds
* 1 tbl flax seeds
* 1 tbl chia seeds
* 1 inch slice fresh ginger
* 1 cup spinach
* 1 frozen banana

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
