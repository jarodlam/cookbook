## Method
* In a jar, add:
    * 1 cup oats
* Assorted dry ingredients:
    * Chia seeds
    * Cacao nibs
    * Goji berries
    * Sultanas
    * Dried apricot
* Then add:
    * ~1 cup (cover dry ingredients) milk
    * ~1 cup (3 large scoops) yoghurt
* Mix thoroughly, add fresh soft fruit on top:
    * Grapes
    * Berries
    * Mango
    * Peach
    * Nectarine
