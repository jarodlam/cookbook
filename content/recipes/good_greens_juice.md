### Ingredients

* 4 leaves romaine lettuce
* 1/2 lemon, peeled
* 1/2 cucumber
* 1/2 apple, peeled
* 1/2 cup chopped pineapple, peeled and cored
* 1/2 cup basil

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
