### Ingredients

* 1 1/2 cups coconut water
* 1 cup chopped pineapple, peeled and cored
* 1/2 cucumber
* 1 cup or 3-4 leaves romaine lettuce
* 3 ice cubes

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
