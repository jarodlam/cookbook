### Ingredients

* 1/2 cup canned coconut milk
* 1 cup chopped pineapple, peeled and cored
* 1/2 cup or 2 kale leaves
* 1 inch slice fresh ginger

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
