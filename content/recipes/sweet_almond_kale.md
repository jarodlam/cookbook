### Ingredients

* 1 cup kale
* 1 1/2 cups almond milk
* 1/2 banana (fresh or frozen)
* 1/2 cup mango (fresh or frozen)
* 1/4 tsp cinnamon

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
