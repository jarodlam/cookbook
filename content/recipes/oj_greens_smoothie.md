### Ingredients

* 1 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
* 1 carrot
* 1 inch slice fresh ginger
* 1 inch slice fresh turmeric or 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
* 1/2 lemon, peeled
* 1 tbl raw honey
* 1 cup or 4 leaves romaine lettuce

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
