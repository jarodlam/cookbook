### Ingredients

* 1 cup almond milk
* 1/2 pear
* 1/4 avocado, peeled
* 1 packed cup spinach
* 1/4 cup coconut water
* 1 tsp chia seeds
* 1 scoop pea protein powder (or hemp protein or brown rice protein)
* 1 cup pure water

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
