### Ingredients

* 1 1 1/2 cups reduced fat milk
* 1/2 cup reduced fat natural yoghurt
* 1 frozen banana
* 3/4 cup frozen blueberries
* 1 tbl honey
* 1 tbl LSA (optional)

### Method

* Blend all ingredients in a blender until smooth.
