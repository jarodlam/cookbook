## Ingredients
* Pre-prepared rempah
* Pineapple cubes
* Coconut milk
* Coconut cream
* Prawns

## Method
1. Fry rempah in lots of oil.
2. Add pinapple cubes and coconut milk. Simmer.
3. Add prawns and coconut cream. Cook for a few minutes only, do not overcook the prawns.
