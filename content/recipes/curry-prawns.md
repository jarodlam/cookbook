## Ingredients
* Pre-prepared rempah
* Pineapple cubes
* Coconut milk
* Prawns

## Method
1. Fry rempah in lots of oil.
2. Add pinapple cues and 2nd round coconut milk. Simmer.
3. Add prawns and 1st round coconut milk. Simmer.
