### Ingredients

* 1 1/2 cups almond milk 
* 1/2 cup blueberries
* 1 pack (about 3.5 oz) frozen unsweetened acai
* 1 cup spinach
* 1/4 avocado, peeled
* 1 tsp chia seeds
* 1 scoop pea protein

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
