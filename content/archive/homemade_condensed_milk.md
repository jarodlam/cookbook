### Ingredients

* 80g water
* 90g butter
* 110g full cream milk powder
* 150g raw sugar

### Method

* Insert butterfly and add ingredients.  Cook 6mins/90 deg/sp 4.

### Note

* You can double the recipe and cook for 10 mins.
