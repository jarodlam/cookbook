### Ingredients

* 1.5 cups frozen mango flesh
* 1/3 cup goji berries
* 50g plain Greek yoghurt
* 1.5 cups water
* 1tbl coconut oil
* 1tbl agave
* 1/2 tsp maca powder
* Pinch of salt

### Method

* Blend all ingredients in a blender until smooth.
