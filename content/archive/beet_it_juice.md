### Ingredients

* 2 Beetroots
* 1 lemon, peeled
* 1 1/2 inch slice fresh ginger
* 1 carrot
* 1 cup or 3 leaves kale (stem included) 

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
