### Ingredients

* 1 cup coconut water
* 1 cup chopped pineapple, peeled and cored
* 3 leaves romaine lettuce
* 2 leaves kale
* 1/2 lime, peeled
* 1/4 cup coriander leaves

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
