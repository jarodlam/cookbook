### Ingredients

* 300 grams milk per serve
* instant coffee to taste
* sugar to taste

### Method

* Add all ingredients to the bowl and heat 4 minutes, 70/75*, speed 3.
* Now froth your milk by blending 20 seconds, speed 8.
