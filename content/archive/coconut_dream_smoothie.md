### Ingredients

* 2 cups kale, stems removed
* 2 cups coconut milk, unsweetened
* 1 cup mango
* 1 cup pineapple
* 1 frozen banana
* 2 tbl coconut flakes, unsweetened

### Method

* Blend all ingredients in a blender until smooth.
