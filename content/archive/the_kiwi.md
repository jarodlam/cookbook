### Ingredients

* 1/2 cup coconut milk
* 1/2 cup coconut water
* 1 kiwi, peeled
* 1/4 avocado, peeled
* Juice of 1/2 lime
* 1/2 cup spinach 

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
