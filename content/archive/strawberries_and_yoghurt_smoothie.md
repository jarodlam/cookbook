### Ingredients

* 1 cup frozen strawberries
* 1/2 cup reduced fat Greek yoghurt
* 1 frozen banana
* 1 cup water
* 2 tsp Acai powder
* 2 tsp chia seeds

### Method

* Blend all ingredients in a blender until smooth.
