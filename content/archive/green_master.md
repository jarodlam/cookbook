### Ingredients

* 1 cup or 3 leaves kale
* 1/4 green apple
* 1/2 lemon, peeled
* 1 inch slice fresh ginger
* 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
* 1 tbl real maple syrup
* 1/2 cup water
* 1/2 cup freshly made apple juice

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
