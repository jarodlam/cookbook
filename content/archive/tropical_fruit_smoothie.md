### Ingredients

* 1 frozen banana
* 1 cup frozen mango flesh
* 1 cup frozen pineapple slices
* 1.5 cups water
* 1tbl agave
* 1tbl coconut sugar
* 2tsp chia or hemp seeds

### Method

* Blend all ingredients in a blender until smooth.
