### Ingredients

* 4 stalks celery
* 1/2 cucumber
* 2 leaves kale
* 1 leaf chard, any variety (with stem)
* 1 handful of spinach
* 1/2 cup parsley
* 1/2 lemon, peeled
* 1 inch slice ginger

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
