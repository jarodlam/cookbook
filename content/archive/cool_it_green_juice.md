### Ingredients

* 1/2 apple, peeled and cored
* 1/2 lime, peeled
* 4 large romaine lettuce leaves
* 1/2 cucumber
* 3 stalks mint

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
