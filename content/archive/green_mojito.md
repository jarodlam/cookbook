### Ingredients

* 1 cup water
* 1 cup chopped pineapple, peeled and cored
* 1/2 lime, peeled
* 1 cup or 1 big handful of spinach
* Leaves of 2 stalks (about 1/4 cup) mint
* 6 ice cubes 

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
