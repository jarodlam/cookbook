### Ingredients

* 1 cup cold green tea (unsweetened)
* 3/4 cup kale
* 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
* 1/3 cup rolled oats
* 1/2 banana
* 1 medjool date or 1 tbl raw honey

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
