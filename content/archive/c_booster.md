### Ingredients

* 1 orange, peeled and seeded
* 1 cup kale
* 1/2 lemon, peeled
* 1 tbl raw honey
* 1 inch slice fresh ginger, grated into the blender 

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
