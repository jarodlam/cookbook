### Ingredients

* 2 cups fresh spinach
* 2 cups almond milk, unsweetened
* 2 cups cherries, pitted
* 2 cups banana
* 1 tsp cinnamon
* 3 tbl cacao powder

### Method

* Blend all ingredients in a blender until smooth.
