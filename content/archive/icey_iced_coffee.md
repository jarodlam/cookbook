### Ingredients

* 250 grams ice cubes
* 200 grams full cream milk
* 2 tablespoons sugar
* 2 heaped teaspoons instant coffee

### Method

* Add all ingredients to the bowl & blend 3 minutes, speed 6.
* This is lovely served with a scoop of icecream!
