### Ingredients

* â€¢50g lemon juice
* handful of favourite herbs (parsley, sage, basil, coriander, oregano)
* 20g TMX stock
* 20g olive oil
* pinch salt
* 1 garlic clove
* red chilli - optional

### Method

* Add all ingredients to bowl and blitz on Speed 8 for 50 seconds.  
* Marinade chicken thighs for 3-5 hours.
* Bake at 180deg for 20 minutes.
