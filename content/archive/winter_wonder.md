### Ingredients

* 1 cup almond milk
* 1 persimmon
* 1 cup or 1 handful of spinach
* 1/2 tsp cinnamon
* 1/4 tsp cardamom
* 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
* 1 medjool date

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
