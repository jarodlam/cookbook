### Ingredients

* 2 cups kale
* 1/2 yellow apple, peeled and cored
* 1/3 cucumber
* 1 stalk celery
* 1/2 lemon, peeled

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
