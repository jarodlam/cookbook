### Ingredients

* 6 leaves romaine lettuce
* 1/2 cup cilantro (coriander leaves)
* 1/2 lime, peeled
* 1 1/2 cups chopped pineapple, peeled and cored

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
