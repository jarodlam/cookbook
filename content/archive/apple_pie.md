### Ingredients

* 1 apple, peeled
* 1 cup kale
* 1 cup nut milk of choice
* 1/2 tsp cinnamon
* 1/4 lemon, peeled
* 1/4 cup walnuts

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
