### Ingredients

* 1 1/2 cups water
* 1 stalk celery
* 1/2 cucumber
* 1/2 avocado
* 1 cup or 4 leaves romaine lettuce
* 1/3 cup parsley
* 1/2 lemon, peeled
* Pinch of sea salt

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
