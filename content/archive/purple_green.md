### Ingredients

* 1 cup coconut milk
* 1/2 cup fresh or frozen blueberries
* 1 cup spinach
* 1/2 tsp cinnamon
* 1/4 avocado
* 1 scoop pea protein
* Water (optional)

### Method

* Blend in the Thermomix on speed 10 for 1.5 minutes.
