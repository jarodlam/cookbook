Ayam kapitan

  • Date Sun, Dec 6, 1992
  • Source Ah Tai

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Pre-prepared rempah
  • Coconut milk

Method

  1. Marinate chicken pieces with ground rempah.
  2. Fry marinated chicken in a pot on low heat until chicken is cooked.
  3. Add in some coconut milk then serve.
