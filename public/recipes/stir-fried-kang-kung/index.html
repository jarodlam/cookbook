Stir fried kang kung

  • Date Sat, Mar 2, 2019
  • Source Mum

Method

  • Fry aromatics:
    • Diced onion
    • Garlic and ginger
    • Shrimps
    • Cut chilli
  • Add kang kung
  • Fry until wilted, add salt and pepper and sesame oil to taste
