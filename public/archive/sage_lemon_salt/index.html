Sage & Lemon Salt

  • Date Thu, Nov 27, 2014

Ingredients

  • 900g Himalayan salt
  • 2 handful sage leaves
  • zest of 3 lemons

Method

  • Dehydrate sage leaves & lemon zest overnight.
  • Blend in TM (20sec on sp8). Add salt & blend again.
  • store in air tight containers.

Notes

  • Great on roast potatoes.
