Bechamel Sauce

  • Date Sat, Jan 25, 2014
  • Source My Way of Cooking Cookbook

Ingredients

  • 40g butter
  • 40g flour
  • 500g milk
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 2 pinches pepper
  • 2 pinches nutmeg

Method
