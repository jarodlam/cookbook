Spanish Rice

  • Date Sun, Jun 2, 2013
  • Tested Sun, May 19, 2013

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped onion
  • 1 teaspoon crushed garlic
  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 3/4 cup chicken stock
  • 1 cup tinned tomatoes
  • ¼ cup peas
  • ¼ cup corn

Method

    1. Heat oil in a large, non-stick saucepan. Stir in onion and garlic, and cook until tender, about 5 minutes.
    1. Mix rice into saucepan, stirring often. When rice begins to brown, stir in chicken broth, corn, peas and tinned tomatoes. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes, until liquid has been absorbed
